ATTENDENCE MANAGEMENT SYSTEM

The system collects attendance records in the system and manages, stores and processes the data. It generates daily attendance reports that help the faculty to track student's presence with high accuracy and efficiency.

STUDENT RESULT

The system stores the details of every student results automatically. accessible by an administrator or Other Users. Only they can add data into the database.

STUDENT ACHEIVEMENTS

Student achievement measures the amount of academic content a student learns in a determined amount of time. Each grade level has learning goals or instructional standards that educators are required to teach.
